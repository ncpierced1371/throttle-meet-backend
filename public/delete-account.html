User Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and personal data at any time.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email us at delete@drivelinecollective.com with the subject line “Delete My Account”.
  2. Include the email address linked to your account.
  3. We will process your request within 30 days.

Alternatively, if an in-app deletion feature is available, you can delete your account directly in your profile settings.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Account information (name, email, login data).
  • Photos, videos, posts, and location history.
  • Any other personal data stored in the app.

Some data may remain as required for legal or security purposes.
