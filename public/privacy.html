Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 15, 2025

At Driveline Collective LLC, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Throttle Meet.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email, profile photo, and basic app details when you sign up or log in with Facebook or Threads.
  • User Content: Photos, videos, posts, and messages you share within the app.
  • Usage Data: Device information, IP address, and interactions within the app.
  • Location Data: Only if you grant permission, to enable features like route sharing, meets, and navigation.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve app features.
  • To allow users to connect, share, and organize meets and cruises.
  • To enhance safety (real-time location sharing during events).
  • For analytics and feature improvements.

3. Sharing of Information

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • Information may be shared with service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, cloud storage) to operate the app.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety of users.

4. Your Choices

  • You can update your profile or settings at any time.
  • You can disable location services in your device settings.
  • You may delete your account following the instructions below.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active or as required by law.

6. Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your information but cannot guarantee 100% security.

7. Children’s Privacy

Throttle Meet is not intended for children under 13.
